Introducing IdeKreatif

idekreatif is a virtual pinboard service based on social media by uploading photos, images, or adding a link to a blog/website. After adding a pinboard, users can also create categories according to the type of image uploaded.

About Us

idekreatif is a visual discovery engine for finding ideas like recipes, home and style inspiration, and more.

With billions of Pins on Pinterest, you'll always find ideas to spark inspiration. When you find a Pin you like, save it to your board to keep your ideas organized and easy to find. You can also create Pins to share ideas with others on idekreatif.
